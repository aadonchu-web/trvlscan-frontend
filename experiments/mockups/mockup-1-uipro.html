Common questions

The honest answers to the things you're going to ask anyway.

Can't find your question? Email hello@trvlscan.com or message us on Telegram. A real person replies, usually inside an hour.

Is it actually a real airline ticket?

Yes. Tickets are issued through Duffel and IATA-licensed travel partners with a real PNR. You check in at the airline counter exactly like anyone who paid with a card. The only thing different is how the money got there.

Which stablecoins do you accept?

USDT on Tron (TRC-20), USDT on Ethereum, and USDC on Solana, Ethereum and Base. Tron is cheapest and fastest — usually settles in under a minute and costs cents in fees. We default to it unless you pick otherwise.

What if the price changes between booking and paying?

We lock the USDT amount for 12 minutes from the moment you click "Pay". If your transaction confirms inside that window — even if the airline price moves or the FX shifts — you pay exactly what you saw. Outside of it, we'll quote a fresh price.

Do you do refunds?

Cancel within 24 hours of booking and the airline allows a full refund — we send the same amount back to the wallet you paid from, in the same stablecoin. After that, refund rules follow whatever fare class you bought (we show this clearly before checkout).

How do you make money if there are no card fees?

A flat 2.5% margin on top of the airline's fare, baked into the USDT price you see. No hidden FX spread, no booking fee that appears at step 4, no "service charge" surprise. The number on the search result is what hits your wallet.

Do I need a crypto wallet I've never used before?

No. If you already hold USDT or USDC anywhere — Binance, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, OKX, Bybit, a hardware wallet — you can pay from it. Scan the QR, send from your usual app, done. We don't make you sign up for anything new.
